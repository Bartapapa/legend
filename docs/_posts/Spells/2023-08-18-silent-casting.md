<b>D:</b> Instantaneous <b>T:</b> Spell <b>R:</b> n/a

You can cast this spell as part of casting another spell. You add [dice] benefits to casting your spell, that you choose upon casting this spell.
*  You do not need to speak to cast the spell.
*  You do not need to move to cast the spell.
*  The spell doesn't make a sound (although objects it affects will still make sound).
*  The target doesn't feel anything that the spell would make them feel (they still suffer damage).
